About Micro EXE

BUILT ON
PASSION

Micro EXE is a start-up service group founded in December 2018 in Taytay, Rizal. Driven by a genuine love for technology, we provide top-quality computer hardware and software maintenance services to homes, businesses, and organizations.

What started as a small group of tech enthusiasts has grown into a trusted name for computer repair, custom builds, diskless solutions, and digital services across Rizal. We believe that quality work and honest service should be accessible to everyone.

Our team combines technical efficiency with a customer-first approach — ensuring every client's machine, project, and system runs at its absolute best.

Fast Turnaround

We respect your time. Most repairs are diagnosed and resolved within 24–48 hours.

Reliable & Trusted

Quality workmanship with transparent pricing — no surprises, ever.

Dedicated Team

A passionate crew of technicians who genuinely care about the outcome of every job.
